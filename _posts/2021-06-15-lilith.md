# What Lilith is.
Lilith is a Evangelion-inspired server that removes the realm, and instead focuses on fighting Angels from the show in three (or less) player groups. A big motivator for making this server is that I've been wanting to use Skilly's EDU source for a while, but I don't have the skillset to implement everything that's currently in runes. Lilith aims to be to something that is entirely unique from realm, but simotaneously something I can work on leisurely, instead of having to worry about meeting deadlines. I've already tried (and failed) at making a paragraph covering EVERYTHING I'm aiming for, so I'm just going to list every key aspect individually.
## Classes and Equipment
There will be three classes. Shinji, Asuka and Rei, They will be able to use all forms of equipment but as a trade-off all equipment stats will be handled based off percent. This will make it so it's harder and less rewarding to rush a stronger character early on, and also puts more emphasis on what class said item is more suited for. All classes will work fundamentally the same, as in: Each classes ability will put them in their EVA for a short period of time. While inside the EVA, certain stats accustommed to that character will increase substansially until your time runs out. I will get more into abilities in one of the later sections.
## Progression
Instead of progression mainly being held in the realm, Lilith is going to place emphasis on smaller groups fighting individual bosses that are really hard. From Evangelion, Adam has 15 children that are sent to Tokyo-3, and for the most part the only people taking them head on are Shinji, Asuka, and Rei. The end goal is to mimic this head-on feeling, and to compensate, RNG is going to be less prevalent. At least to the extent where you don't have to bust your ass over 1/1000 drop rates. I'm aiming for 1/5 drop rates of items that are on-par with what you're fighting, and 1/25-50 for items that are significantly stronger. One caveat though, with drop rates being that low. Each boss is going to take a solid 10-15 minutes to complete AT LEAST. Obviously there will be factors like health-scaling to make 1 man groups not out of reach, but I want to avoid making bosses heavily grindable like O.G Alvin Realms was.
## Leveling, Maxing, and Abilities
Leveling is going to be handled as SYNC %, which is a 100 level system that maxes your character upon reaching 100% SYNC. EVA's are essentially gods, and if YOU only have 1% SYNC, you can only harness AT MOST 1% of their full power. Leveling SYNC is going to be done solely through killing Adam's children. This does mean you can max SYNC by only fighting Adam's first child, but it will take EXCRUCIATINGLY long. Imagine reaching Level 120 in Project B by only killing beach enemies. This ties into abilities because, I didn't mention this earlier but there is going to be no tiered abilities. Instead of having multiple tiers, the duration you are able to stay in your EVA is dependent on what your SYNC is. When you fight bosses, you are going to be significantly more vulnerable outside your EVA, so you're going to want to take advantage of each ability use. For example, I plan on having one class use HP instead of MP, but in return you get 33% VIT and DEF, and a chance to dodge shots while in the EVA for lets say.. 12 seconds. Even though that is really good, the cooldown would be 60 seconds. Under the metric of each boss taking 10-15 minutes, you are only using your max potential for ~15% of the fight, while being essentially a glass cannon the remaining 85%.
