## ORYX - 
Oryx is a dark god who rules over multiple realms, the realm the game takes place in is just one of the many planes he is attempting to conquest, he is a destroyer, literally formed from hatred and pride, who enjoys slowly taking over realms and doing as he pleases with them.

## ORYX THE MAD GOD -
Simply one of the projections of the true oryx, he has appointed his projection as the ruler of the realm and uses it to suppress all the other beings the realm contains, corrupting them or forcing them under his rule, knowing that the heroes would be summoned (as they have with the other realms he has taken over) he places this projection as a simple test for the hero's seeing if they are truly good enough to warrant him using any more of his ni-infinite dark energy.

## ORYX THE MAD KING - 
The purest projection of oryx's energy, as the most amount of power he can force directly onto a realm, possess the most power that realm has to offer, being able to use any of the elemental types of magic, as well as pure dark energy, it can summon waves of minions using scraps of the left-over powers the true oryx used to create it. after defeating the Mad God, you have been invited directly to battle the God-King, as a test of both your power and the powers the dark god has pushed onto the realm.
Defeating the Projection of Oryx: After finally defeating the God-King, the realm is freed of the dark god's corruption, you return to the nexus of heroes, only to see another realm has opened, oryx has decided that he is done with that realm, and continues on his conquest.

## THE NEXUS -
A gathering place for legendary heroes, it summons heroes from various planes of existence after there deaths and summons them to fight evil across various realms, the heroes are repaid in fame and glory across the "Universal Leaderboards" for their merits, the nexus itself was created by the 16 gods of light, who now can no longer express their power due to strenuous fighting against dark gods.

## HEROES -
Humans (or other beings) who have garnered legendary status on their original realms, after dying in battle (or from other means) they are summoned to the nexus, partaking in an infinite battle against darkness across a near-infinite amount of realms that are created as fast as they are taken over or destroyed by dark gods, they are selected by the nexus depending on the amount of good merit they achieve in their original realms.

## DEATH - 
While fighting against darkness, millions of heroes have lost theirs lives, after dying the "soul" of that hero reincarnates into a child on a light realm, dying only to be summoned by the nexus again, the heroes, when summoned to the nexus remember all their past lives and experiences fighting the darkness, some heroes fall into a deep depression after countless deaths, some are only more motivated to keep fighting, seeing if they can finally end the infinite cycle of death, reincarnation, being summoned, fighting, and dying again.

## THE FORGOTTEN -
Heroes whose minds have broken fighting the darkness, after fighting for hundreds of reincarnations over tens of thousands of years, their minds have shattered, either allowing their souls to fall into the "Ather" or lying around in the nexus for uncountable amounts of time, the ones not able to enter the "Ather" sit around and wish for death to finally grip them, some commit suicide and find themselves back in the nexus a single reincarnation later, falling into a cycle of what they perceive as torture.

## LEGENDARY HEROES -
Heroes a cut above the rest, they have saved thousands of realms from demise, and slain hundreds of thousands of dark entities, venturing across space and time to slay enemies to the gods of light and the nexus. After fighting for all this time, they achieve the ability to enter the "Ather", finally freeing themselves from the infinite cycle, yet in their place, a fragment of pure violence and reverence for the gods of light remains, going on a killing spree in a mindless attempt to destroy darkness.

## FRAGMENTS -
The fragments left over when legendary heroes enter the "Ather" beings of pure light and killing intent, who stop at nothing to destroy darkness, no matter what is in their way, they are completely avoided, as they are not known to discern friend from foe, recklessly destroying all that stand before it, identifying them as enemies of the nexus.

## THE FALLEN - 
Heroes broke by the cycle of the nexus, they completely abandon it. Eventually becoming corrupt, they have become mindless beings who only know destruction, both similar and the complete opposite to fragments, they will kill anything that enters their sight, only stopping when they have died or when they finally fade away.

## THE ATHER -
The true "god" a structure of pure energy, the size of millions of realms, the only thing that stops the dark gods from destroying the nexus and the cycle of reincarnation, it created the realms and the light gods, it constantly creates new realms over time, and has enough power to keep creating them for multiple millennia, the end-goal of the dark gods is to widdle it down over millions or even trillions of years. Even a single legendary hero gives the "Ather" enough power to keep creating realms for thousands of years, leading to the dark gods creating the fallen, to counteract Legendary heroes.

## NEXUS CRIER -
An artificial being created by the light gods to give heroes a sense of familiarity, it is a lifeless being who repeats phrases at set timings to give the heroes hope, trying to hold up a conversation with him doesn't hold up quite as well though, as he does not respond to direct speech, attempting to kill the Nexus Crier or remove him for his post simply recreates him and returns him to his original position.

## MARKET -
Another artificial being runs the market, modeled from various merchants from the thousands of realms, he doesn't talk, only allowing the heroes to put up legendary artifacts from their ventures and sell them to other heroes for "Fame" put on an infinite loop by the gods of light, he never sleeps, never moves, only stocking his shop with artifacts and showing them to other heroes interested in the market, he does take a cut of the fame generated by the heroes selling items, using it to keep himself powered and allowing him to sell more artifacts.

## FAME -
Both a form of currency and what allows heroes to keep reincarnating, it works similar to coal burning in a furnace, powering the very essence of the heroes and the nexus, without fame there are no heroes, without heroes fame can't be generated, fame also powers the nexus, all of the artificial beings inside of it, and also the divine power that stops darkness from leaking in. When a hero dies a large portion of the fame is directly forced into the reserves of the nexus, after reincarnation, fame is given to that hero's said reincarnation, allowing him to trade with it and access the Universal Leaderboard.

## THE LEADERBOARD -
A ranking of sorts made by light gods to incentivize competition between heroes, the higher on the universal leaderboard a hero places, the more "Fame" they gain both from their time spent fighting the darkness and the deeds they achieve. Heroes high on the leaderboards are recognized by the nexus, giving them incentive and prestige on the reincarnations. The leaderboard changes constantly, allowing rookie heroes to eventually overcome their predecessors, legendary heroes are created when a hero achieves so much in one reincarnation that the nexus can no longer allow them to be a mundane existence, given a title, most go on to either fight for thousands of years or fade into the "Ather" as they serve as a great power for it.

## THE ADMINS -
Heroes who have fought for longer than all other heroes, the nexus allows them to take up leadership positions, managing the other heroes and directly controlling the realms the heroes access. While some grow drunk on their powers, many attempts to help the new heroes and reward the old and dedicated, the nexus is still alive today because of dedicated admins.

## SIMULATIONS -
As tests or challenges to keep other heroes on their feet, the admins will occasionally create simulations of enemies, dying to the simulations don't directly kill the heroes, or hurt their fame, but it definitly hurts their pride. Admins achieve this by using fame and controlling the power of the nexus, coupled with their past experiences fighting the darkness.

## LEVELING - 
When a hero reincarnates into the nexus, be it their first time or any time after that, they are weakened, directly summoning a top condition hero would require too much power from the nexus, so the heroes must go into the realm to bolster their power and gain "Experience" from fighting enemies, also gaining fame for their deeds. When they reach the highest point their bodies can handle without runes or potions, they are known as level twenties.

## POTIONS - 
Potions are the creations of the Gods of light, they programmed the information directly into their creator, the "Ather" so any realm created by the "Ather" contains them, they created them to allow the heroes to fight with more valor against the gods of darkness and their minions, drinking the potion, depending on the type directly reconstructs the heroes body. A hero who has drunk enough potions to not feel their effects anymore are known as Maxed.

## POTION OF DEFENSE -
When drank, Thickens the bones and makes them harder, allowing heroes to take significantly more damage before returning to the nexus to heal.

## POTION OF ATTACK -
When drank, Increases the explosive power of the muscles, allowing heroes to strike down enemies far easier.

## POTION OF LIFE -
When drank, Increases both the amount of blood and the strength of the heroes body, allowing them to be pierced, crushed, and slashed without losing nearly as much blood, also directly decreasing the speed at which they accumulate fatigue.

## THE RAID NPC -
An unknown entity, even the gods of light didn't know how he got into the nexus, but he didn't seem hostile, so they didn't remove him. He is known to be generally helpful, although he doesn't like to talk that much.

## THE RAID PORTAL -
A strange formation of stones created by the Raid Npc, after recieving some power from the nexus it can be used to open strange portals to other planes of existance

## RAIDS - 
Planes of existence that have been corrupted or conquested by dark gods or other forms of life, after warping to them, the heroes can only try their hand at saving what is left of the planes or die trying like the thousands of heroes before them, all of the planes the raid portal leads to are ranked at a difficult far higher than that of the normal realm, the gods of light considered them impossible to clear until a legendary hero named "Paracosm" re-conquered and annihilated plane all by himself, he is now known as the "King of conquest" as he has "saved" thousands of planes with his scarlet gungnir.

## KING OF CONQUEST, PARACOSM -
A legendary hero who hasn't yet fallen into the "Ather" he uses a weapon called the scarlet gungnir, this legendary weapon has been covered in the blood of hundreds of thousands of evil entities and is now capable of damaging even godly entities, after saving thousands of planes, he is renown as one of the strongest heroes.

## TITAN, THE MOUNTAIN SPLITTER - 
A Legendary Hero who has become an admin, now helping new heroes and coaching them into future legendary heroes, when he was still a hero, his weapon "The Titan Slayer" could cleave entire mountains in two, he decided to become an admin after thousands of years of fighting, after attempting to conquest a plane with paracosm and the other legendary heroes, they ran into an unknown entity, who took many of Titans friends and one of his arms, forcing him to retire, no longer being able to wield his legendary weapon.
